Finishing Room

The finishing room, located next to the showroom, is used and on display for the final "fit and finish" of vehicles that are nearly ready for delivery or sale. Separate from the workshop that handles major work, finishing and installation activites include but are not limited to:

  • carpet
  • steel and wood trim
  • glass and windshields
  • seats
  • lighting
  • buffing and waxing
  • tires
  • vacuuming and cleaning

Finishing work such as the above is a part of our overall restoration process to deliver world-class vehicles.
