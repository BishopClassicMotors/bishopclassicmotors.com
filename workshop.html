Workshop

Body, drivetrain, and paint work occurs at our workshop. Located separately from the showroom and finishing room, services also include but are not limited to:

  • welding
  • sheet metal forming
  • composites fabrication
  • specialized woodwork
  • electronics installation
  • engine rebuilding
  • general repair

Major work such as the above is a part of our overall restoration process to deliver world-class vehicles.
